Analytics & User Experience Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve the website's performance and user experience through analytics and user behavior analysis.

Services: Google Analytics, Hotjar

Google Analytics - Data collected: Page views, time spent on site, bounce rate, traffic sources, device information (anonymized)

Retention: Google Analytics - 26 months, Hotjar - 365 days

Purpose: Website optimization, user experience improvement, identifying usability issues, understanding user behavior patterns

About Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics & User Experience Cookies: Help us understand how you use our website and improve the user experience through behavior analysis
