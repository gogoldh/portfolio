About Me

I'm Lukas D'Haese — a Digital Experience Design student blending front-end and back-end development with a sharp eye for UI/UX design. I love creating seamless, engaging digital experiences that balance form and function.

I also have a deep passion for photography and post-processing in tools like Lightroom and Photoshop. Capturing and shaping visuals helps me stay creative and sharp-eyed in my design work.

When I’m not coding or designing, I’m spending my time at Circolito — training in acrobatics (Acro Porte), where trust, timing, and creativity come together in motion, or performing with Gen C.

  • Location: Belgium
  • Specialties: UX/UI Design, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Creative Coding, Motion Design
