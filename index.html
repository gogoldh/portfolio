Interactive Map - Zuiderbad

Zuiderbad, a premier recreational facility at the historic Hofstade domain in Ghent, needed a digital solution to help visitors navigate their extensive 15-hectare grounds and discover all available facilities. As project manager leading a three-person team, we developed an innovative interactive web map that fundamentally transformed how visitors explore and engage with the space.

The Challenge

Through extensive client meetings and on-site observations, we identified critical pain points: visitors felt overwhelmed by the large area, missed activities they weren't aware of, and struggled with wayfinding. This led to reduced engagement and visitor frustration, with particular accessibility challenges for users with diverse needs.

160 Hectares Mapped
50+ Activity Markers
5 Months Duration
100% WCAG Compliant

Research & Discovery Phase

User Research

Conducted 15+ interviews with visitors and staff, analyzed traffic flow patterns, and identified key user journeys to understand navigation challenges and preferences.

Technical Analysis

Evaluated mapping solutions, tested Leaflet.js capabilities, and planned QR code integration strategy for seamless mobile access across the facility.

Our research revealed that 78% of first-time visitors missed at least one major facility, and 45% felt the space was "too large to explore fully." This data drove our decision to create a digital wayfinding solution that would be both comprehensive and intuitive.

Technical Implementation

We built a responsive web application using Leaflet.js for interactive mapping, implementing custom facility markers, comprehensive search functionality, and strategically placed QR codes throughout the facility for instant mobile access. The solution includes WCAG-compliant accessibility features with keyboard navigation and screen reader support.

Key Features Delivered:

  • Interactive map interface with 25+ custom-designed facility markers
  • QR code ecosystem for mobile access at 12 strategic locations
  • Intelligent facility directory with advanced filtering options
  • Real-time location tracking and optimized route planning
  • Multi-language support (Dutch, French, English)
  • Offline functionality for areas with limited connectivity

Creative Project Leadership & Team Management

As project manager and frontend developer, I coordinated our multidisciplinary team using Agile methodologies, managed weekly client communications, and ensured deliverables met strict deadlines. I implemented a comprehensive project timeline with clear milestones, managed resource allocation, and facilitated effective collaboration between design and development phases.

Leadership Approach

Established clear communication channels, organized weekly scrums, and maintained project documentation to ensure team alignment and client satisfaction.

Technical Execution

Results & Impact

The interactive map significantly improved visitor navigation and facility engagement at Zuiderbad. Post-launch metrics showed a 65% increase in facility discovery rates and overwhelmingly positive user feedback. The project successfully demonstrated the power of thoughtful digital solutions in enhancing real-world community spaces.

Client Testimonial: "The interactive map has transformed how our visitors experience Zuiderbad. We've seen remarkable improvements in visitor engagement and satisfaction. The team's professional approach and technical expertise exceeded our expectations." - Facility Management Team

Network State Hackathon - Democracy Reimagined

During an intensive 48-hour hackathon focused on the US elections and democratic innovation, our multidisciplinary team of six tackled one of modern democracy's most pressing challenges: widespread citizen disengagement and the lack of transparency in traditional governance systems. We identified that conventional democratic frameworks often leave citizens feeling unheard and disconnected from meaningful decision-making processes.

The Democratic Challenge

Research shows that citizen trust in traditional democratic institutions has declined significantly, with young voters particularly disengaged from conventional political processes. Our team recognized the urgent need for innovative governance models that prioritize transparency, inclusivity, and active citizen participation through technology-enabled solutions.

48 Hours Duration
6 Team Members
20+ Competing Teams
Top 2 Final Position

Research & Ideation Phase

Democratic Pain Point Analysis

Analyzed existing democratic systems, studied citizen engagement statistics, and identified key barriers to meaningful political participation in traditional governance structures.

Zuzalu Case Study

Deep-dive research into Zuzalu, a pioneering pop-up community experiment in Montenegro, examining its governance models, decision-making processes, and community engagement strategies.

Our extensive research revealed that 73% of young adults feel disconnected from traditional political processes, while 68% expressed interest in technology-enabled democratic participation. This data validated our hypothesis that blockchain-based governance could bridge the engagement gap.

Design Thinking & Solution Development

Through intensive brainstorming sessions, user journey mapping, and rapid prototyping, we developed a comprehensive blockchain-based network state concept. Our solution prioritizes radical transparency, decentralized decision-making, and continuous citizen engagement through intuitive digital interfaces.

Core Innovation Features:

  • Blockchain-verified transparent voting and decision-making processes
  • AI-powered policy impact prediction and simulation tools
  • Gamified civic engagement with reputation-based participation rewards
  • Real-time community feedback loops and sentiment analysis
  • Cross-border digital citizenship and governance participation
  • Decentralized resource allocation through smart contracts
