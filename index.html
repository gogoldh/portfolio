Interactive Map - Zuiderbad

Project Impact

The Challenge We Solved

Zuiderbad, a premier recreational facility at the historic Hofstade domain in Mechelen, needed a digital solution to help visitors navigate their extensive 15-hectare grounds and discover all available facilities. As a team, we developed an innovative interactive web map that fundamentally transformed how visitors explore and engage with the space.

Through extensive client meetings and on-site observations, we identified critical pain points: visitors felt overwhelmed by the large area, missed activities they weren't aware of, and struggled with wayfinding. This led to reduced engagement and visitor frustration, with particular accessibility challenges for users with diverse needs.

How We Delivered This Impact

1. Research & Discovery

Conducted 15+ interviews with visitors and staff, analyzed traffic flow patterns, and identified key user journeys. Research revealed many first-time visitors missed major facilities, validating our approach.

2. Technical Solution Design

Evaluated mapping solutions, implemented Leaflet.js with custom facility markers, and planned QR code integration strategy for seamless mobile access across the facility.

3. Team Leadership & Project Management

As project manager and frontend developer, I coordinated our multidisciplinary team using Agile methodologies, managed weekly client communications, and ensured deliverables met strict deadlines.

Technical Implementation & Features

We built a responsive web application using Leaflet.js for interactive mapping, implementing custom facility markers, comprehensive search functionality, and strategically placed QR codes throughout the facility for instant mobile access. The solution includes WCAG-compliant accessibility features with keyboard navigation and screen reader support.

Key Features Delivered:

  • Interactive map interface with 25+ custom-designed facility markers
  • QR code ecosystem for mobile access at 12 strategic locations
  • Intelligent facility directory with advanced filtering options
  • Real-time location tracking and optimized route planning
  • Multi-language support (Dutch, French, English)
  • Offline functionality for areas with limited connectivity

✨ Proven Results & Long-term Impact

The solution exceeded expectations: Successfully delivered a complete digital wayfinding system that makes all facility locations easily discoverable through intuitive navigation. The project demonstrates effective team leadership, technical problem-solving, and ability to create practical solutions that enhance user experience in real-world environments.

Why This Project Matters: This showcases my ability to lead complex technical projects from conception to delivery, manage cross-functional teams effectively, and create digital solutions that solve tangible user problems while maintaining high technical standards. The successful collaboration and on-time delivery prepared me for professional project management challenges.

The Challenge We Tackled

During an intensive 48-hour hackathon focused on the US elections and democratic innovation, our multidisciplinary team of six tackled one of modern democracy's most pressing challenges: widespread citizen disengagement and the lack of transparency in traditional governance systems.

Research shows that citizen trust in traditional democratic institutions has declined significantly, with young voters particularly disengaged from conventional political processes. Our team recognized the urgent need for innovative governance models that prioritize transparency, inclusivity, and active citizen participation through technology-enabled solutions.

How We Achieved This Success

1. Research & Problem Analysis

Analyzed existing democratic systems and studied citizen engagement statistics. Research revealed 73% of young adults feel disconnected from traditional political processes, validating our blockchain-based approach.

2. Solution Design & Innovation

Through intensive brainstorming and rapid prototyping, we developed a comprehensive blockchain-based network state concept prioritizing transparency, decentralized decision-making, and continuous citizen engagement.

3. UX/UI Leadership & Execution

As UX/UI design lead, I spearheaded user experience strategy, facilitated design sprints, and created high-fidelity prototypes that effectively communicated complex governance concepts under extreme time pressure.

Core Innovation Features Designed:

  • Blockchain-verified transparent voting and decision-making processes
  • AI-powered policy impact prediction and simulation tools
  • Gamified civic engagement with reputation-based participation rewards
  • Real-time community feedback loops and intuitive digital interfaces
  • Cross-border digital citizenship and governance participation
